Moʻo Paʻakai

Reviving traditional salt practices on Maui through learning opportunities.

What is Moʻo Paʻakai?

Community Effort

Moʻo Paʻakai is a project under Kauahea, Inc. that supports the return of Maui salt knowledge and place-based education.

Why Paʻakai?

Paʻakai served many purposes in Hawaiian life, including seasoning, preserving food, healing, cleansing, and ceremony.

Work at Keālia

The project supports restoration of wetland and salt-making spaces while preparing for future cultural and educational use.

Project Goals

Restore places connected to paʻakai production
Support ʻāina based learning
Teach the history and uses of paʻakai
Create a space for future kūpuna

Grounding Values

Aloha ʻĀina

Caring for place through active work and responsibility.

Kuleana

Taking responsibility for cultural practice, people, and place.

Pilina

Building relationships through shared work and learning.
